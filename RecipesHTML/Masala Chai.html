Recipe By:

PriyaPatel

Ingredients:

• Water

• Milk

• Black tea leaves

• Sugar

• Ginger

• Cardamom

• Cinnamon

• Cloves

Instructions:

1. Boil water with spices (ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves). 2. Add tea leaves, simmer. 3. Add milk, bring to boil. 4. Strain into cups, sweeten with sugar. 5. Serve hot.

Masala Chai

Prep time: 15 minutes

Serving size: 2 cups

Ingredients: 8

Masala Chai is a traditional Indian spiced tea made with black tea leaves, milk, and a blend of aromatic spices like ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, sweetened with sugar.
