Recipe By:

NatashaLee

Ingredients:

• Wide rice noodles

• Chicken or beef

• Broccoli

• Egg

• Garlic

• Dark soy sauce

• Oyster sauce

• Sugar

• Vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Stir-fry garlic in oil, add meat. 2. Add noodles, broccoli, sauces, sugar. 3. Push aside, scramble egg. 4. Toss everything until heated through. 5. Serve hot.

Pad See Ew

Prep time: 30 minutes

Serving size: 2 servings

Ingredients: 9

Pad See Ew is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish with wide rice noodles, tender chicken or beef, broccoli, and egg, flavored with a sweet and savory soy-based sauce.
