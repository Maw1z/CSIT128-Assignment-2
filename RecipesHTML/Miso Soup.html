Recipe By:

SarahChang

Ingredients:

• Dashi stock

• Miso paste

• Tofu

• Seaweed

• Green onions

Instructions:

1. Bring dashi stock to boil. 2. Dissolve miso paste in stock. 3. Add tofu, seaweed, simmer briefly. 4. Garnish with green onions. 5. Serve hot.

Miso Soup

Prep time: 15 minutes

Serving size: 4 servings

Ingredients: 5

Traditional Japanese soup made with dashi stock and miso paste, filled with tofu cubes, seaweed, and garnished with chopped green onions. A comforting and nutritious dish.
