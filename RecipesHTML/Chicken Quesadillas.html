Recipe By:

CarlosHernandez

Ingredients:

• Chicken breast

• Flour tortillas

• Cheese

• Bell peppers

• Onion

• Jalapeno

• Cumin

• Paprika

• Salt

Instructions:

1. Cook chicken, shred. 2. Layer cheese, chicken, veggies on tortilla. 3. Fold over, cook until cheese melts. 4. Serve hot with salsa and sour cream.

Chicken Quesadillas

Prep time: 30 minutes

Serving size: 4 quesadillas

Ingredients: 9

Mexican quesadillas filled with tender chicken, cheese, bell peppers, and onions, seasoned with cumin and paprika, grilled until crispy and served with salsa and sour cream.
