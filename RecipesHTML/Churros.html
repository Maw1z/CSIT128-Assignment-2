Recipe By:

CarlosGonzalez

Ingredients:

• Water

• Butter

• Sugar

• Salt

• All-purpose flour

• Eggs

• Vegetable oil

• Cinnamon sugar

Instructions:

1. Boil water, butter, sugar, salt. 2. Stir in flour until dough forms. 3. Beat in eggs until smooth. 4. Pipe into hot oil, fry until golden. 5. Roll in cinnamon sugar. 6. Serve warm.

Churros

Prep time: 45 minutes

Serving size: 16 churros

Ingredients: 8

Traditional Mexican fried dough pastry, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, rolled in cinnamon sugar. Served hot as a popular snack or dessert.
