Recipe By:

GiuseppeRossi

Ingredients:

• Tomatoes

• Fresh mozzarella

• Basil leaves

• Olive oil

• Balsamic glaze

• Salt

• Pepper

Instructions:

1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella. 2. Arrange on plate with basil leaves. 3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. 4. Season with salt and pepper. 5. Serve immediately.

Caprese Salad

Prep time: 15 minutes

Serving size: 2 servings

Ingredients: 7

A simple Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Light and flavorful.
