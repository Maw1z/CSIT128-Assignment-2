Recipe By:

ChrisJohnson

Ingredients:

• Cake flour

• Milk

• Eggs

• Sugar

• Baking powder

• Vanilla extract

• Butter

Instructions:

1. Separate egg whites and yolks. 2. Beat yolks with sugar until pale. 3. Mix in milk, vanilla, melted butter. 4. Sift in flour and baking powder, mix until smooth. 5. Whip egg whites until stiff peaks form. 6. Gently fold whites into batter. 7. Cook in rings on low heat, flipping carefully. 8. Serve hot, topped with syrup and fruits.
