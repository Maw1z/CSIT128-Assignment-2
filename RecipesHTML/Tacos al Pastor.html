Recipe By:

MariaGarcia

Ingredients:

• Pork shoulder

• Ancho chilies

• Pineapple

• Onion

• Cilantro

• Corn tortillas

• Lime

• Garlic

• Oregano

Instructions:

1. Marinate pork with chilies, pineapple, spices. 2. Grill or roast until tender. 3. Slice thinly, serve in tortillas with onion, cilantro. 4. Garnish with lime wedges.

Tacos al Pastor

Prep time: 1 hour

Serving size: 4 tacos

Ingredients: 9

Traditional Mexican street tacos made with marinated pork shoulder, grilled with pineapple and spices, served on corn tortillas with onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
