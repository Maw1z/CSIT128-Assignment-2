Recipe description

A traditional Italian breakfast pastry that combines the flakiness of a croissant with the richness of brioche dough. This delectable treat is often filled with a variety of fillings such as custard, chocolate, or jam. Served warm, it has a golden, buttery exterior with a soft, airy interior. Perfect for enjoying with a cappuccino, it offers a delightful start to the day with its balance of sweetness and texture, making it a beloved morning indulgence in Italy.
